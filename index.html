Privacy Policy for NutriLog

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NutriLog. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and name to create and manage your account.
  • Nutrition Data: Food logs, meal descriptions, calorie intake, and macronutrient information you enter into the app.
  • Goal Settings: Your personal nutrition goals (daily calories, protein, carbs, and fat targets).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the NutriLog service
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Store your nutrition logs and goals
  • Analyze food descriptions using AI to calculate nutritional values
  • Improve our app and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication and sign-in
  • Supabase: For secure database storage of your account and nutrition data
  • OpenAI: To process food descriptions and calculate nutritional information

These services have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their terms.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your nutrition data is private and only accessible by you through your account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data

8. Account Deletion

If you would like your account and all associated data deleted, please email us at jeffrey.vanoosterwijk@gmail.com with your request. We will process your deletion as soon as possible.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
